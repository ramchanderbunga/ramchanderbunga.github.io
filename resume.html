My Design Process
Research
The research stage of the process entails gathering information about the user, the business goals, and the competition. This information can be collected through various methods such as user interviews, surveys, and analytics data.
Analysis
Following the collection of research data, the next stage is analysis, which entails synthesising the data and identifying user needs, pain points, and goals.
Design
The third stage is design, which is when the actual design work starts. This stage entails creating wireframes, prototypes, and visual designs that meet user needs while also achieving business objectives.
Testing
Testing comes after the designs have been completed. This entails putting the designs through user testing to identify usability issues and gather feedback.
Iteration
The designs are refined and improved based on the feedback received. This testing and refining process is repeated until a final design is reached.
Implementation
The final stage of the process is implementation, which involves turning the designs into a functional product. Working with developers to ensure that the final product meets the design specifications is part of this stage.
